Hey, welcome!

I'm a PhD student at ENS at the crossroads of Machine Learning and Cryptology

I'm doing a PhD in Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning at ENS and INRIA under the supervision of David Pointcheval and Francis Bach. I'm also the leader of the Cryptography Team at OpenMined, a community building practical tools for private AI, and a co-founder of Arkhn which builds concrete solutions for hospitals. I publish regularly about my work and readings on my blog.
